MQtest.io

A simple tool to help identify which media queries your device responds to when using ‘width=device-width.’

This test isn’t about what media que­ries your device can see. In­stead it shows which dimen­sions your de­vice will res­pond to. You could think of these mea­sures as the max-widths and max-heights in a mobile first sty­le­sheet for the device you are testing.
