A simple tool to help identify which media queries your device responds to.

This test isn’t about what media que­ries your device can or cannot see. In­stead it shows you which dimen­sions your de­vice will res­pond to when using ‘width=device-width.’ You could think of these mea­sures as the max-widths and max-heights in a sty­le­sheet for the device you are testing.

Device responds to

  • Your browser doesn't support media queries :(
  • JavaScript is turned off or not supported.
